potato

In the villages, season after season, the community grows potatoes, repeating and maintaining cultivation processes. The residents harvest their potatoes, eat them together and share them with their neighbours.

 


The art publication is the result of observing potato harvesting practices in a Komi village. The artist and my mother, Anzhelika Van-In, made graphic sketches by talking with neighbours. Based on their stories, we has compiled together a series of memories and impressions of villagers about the potato harvesting process. 

This zine was developed as part of a series of field studies on women's cultivation practices in the village environment, becoming a collaborative project between mother and daughter. The zine consists of five interrelated groups: collection, community, storage, preparation.

Artists: Anzhelika Van-In 
Publishers: Nika Grigorian, Nickolas Daniel da Silva
