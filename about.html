BIO

My interdisciplinary practice centres on themes of representation, with a particular focus on rural spaces, exploring rural/village practices, collectivism/public art and working with communities. Through ongoing experimentation and applied research methods, I create formats for alternative forms of sociality with human and non-human bodies.

Based between Berlin and St Petersburg, I am also often in HfK Bremen, where I am writing my doctoral thesis on the cultivation practices of Eastern European women in the rural spaces. Furthermore, I spend some time in villages of Russia, Armenia and Germany.
