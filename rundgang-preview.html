 Rundgang Preview

2021 / Group project / Exhibition

In a team with my university colleagues we organised an exhibition “Rundgang Preview” in Haus der Statistik.

The exhibition “Rundgang Preview” in Haus der Statistik was dedicated to the final works of our Master's programme “Art in Context”. It gave an impression of the range of the institute's artistic approaches, contemporary artistic issues within and outside the academy.

As part of the exhibitio a digital archive was created: https://preview.kunstimkontext.net/
