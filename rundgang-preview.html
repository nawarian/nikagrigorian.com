 Rundgang Preview

2021 / Group project / Exhibition

The exhibition "Rundgang Preview" at the Haus der Statistik was dedicated to the final projects of our master's program "Art in Context", in which Grigorian graduated in 2021. It gave an impression of the range of artistic approaches of the Institute, of current artistic issues within and outside the Academy. As part of the exhibition, a digital archive was created: https://preview.kunstimkontext.net/

Grigorian participated in the curatorial development of the exhibition and presented her artistic research work "Kokvitskaya Gora".
