BIO

Nika Grigorian is a russian interdisciplinary researcher and artist. Her practice focuses on themes of representation, with particular attention to rural spaces, the study of rural/village practices, collectivism/public art and working with communities. She lives in Berlin but is also often in Bremen, where she is working on her doctoral thesis on Eastern European women's cultivation practices in rural spaces. She also spends some time in villages in Russia, Armenia and Germany for research and joy.
