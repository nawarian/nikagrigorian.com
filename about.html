BIO

Nika Grigorian is an interdisciplinary artist, curator and researcher, based in Berlin. At the core of her practice is the method of field research. By researching out places, events and people that remain hidden from general view, she seeks to liberate knowledge from a one-dimensional narrative and create exchange.

Her experiences as a journalist shape her methodological framework. In 2021, Grigorian completed her studies at the Institute "Kunst Im Kontext" with her work "Kokvitskaya Gora - the process of standing for community rights", in which she dealt with questions surrounding the sustainable development of isolated village structures in the Komi Republic (northern Russia). 

Born and raised in Russia, she spent her childhood between the urban space of St.Petersburg and the Komi countryside, which influenced her artistic visions.
